Lasagna

Description

Traditional italian dish made of pasta, bolognese sauce and fresh mozzarella cheese baked in a casserole dish.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Bolognese sauce
  • Mozzarella di bufala

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Grease baking dish.
  3. Layer sauce and pasta, adding chunks of mozzarella every couple of layers.
  4. Bake for aproximmately 30 minutes, or until edges are golden brown and bubbly.
